About

Chess.Ai leverages the power of ChatGPT for natural language processing
and the Stockfish API for advanced chess analysis, providing users with
insightful game evaluations and interactive experiences on the website.

My name is Ezekiel Norman, and I am a software developer who harbors a passion for playing chess. Often, I found myself pondering how to enhance my skills when many existing engines simply show the best move without elucidating why it's the optimal choice. Driven by this curiosity and a desire to analyze my own mistakes, I embarked on a journey to create a tool tailored mainly to my own needs and interests.

Utilizing chess.js and chessboard.js, I crafted this innovative tool that seamlessly combines the linguistic capabilities of ChatGPT with the robust analysis of the Stockfish engine. The result is a user-friendly platform that offers insightful analysis based on the current state of the board (FEN) and Stockfish's best move recommendations. Whether you're a novice or a seasoned player, this tool aims to deepen your understanding of the game, enhancing both strategy and enjoyment.
